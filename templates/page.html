Design & Visual Thinking

Design is where my attention to detail and cultural awareness come together. I focus on layout, color, accessibility, and overall user experience to create visuals that feel intuitive and meaningful. My background in Asian Studies influences my design decisions by shaping how I think about symbolism, minimalism, and cross-cultural communication. I aim to create interfaces that not only look good but also respect diverse perspectives and make information easier for users to navigate.
