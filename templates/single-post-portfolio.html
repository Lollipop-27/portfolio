The Portfolio Site was created to showcase both my design and technical skills for graduate school applications and professional opportunities. The project’s purpose was to present a comprehensive view of my design process, from ideation and wireframing to final implementation, while highlighting accessibility, usability, and visual design principles. The site also serves as a professional portfolio that communicates my personal design philosophy and approach to problem-solving.

Lesson learned:
This project taught me how to build cleaner, more organized code, design with consistency, and solve problems step-by-step. It strengthened both my technical skills and my confidence in creating a polished, functional website.

Design process

Wireframes

Wireframing began as a way to explore the structure of the site and organize content effectively. I sketched out layouts, mapped navigation, and considered the hierarchy of information to visualize how users might move through the pages. Each iteration highlighted potential usability challenges, which I refined through feedback and testing. This process helped shape the high-fidelity designs, ensuring that the final layouts were intuitive, cohesive, and user-focused.

Style guide / colour palette

Creating the style guide was an iterative process aimed at establishing a consistent visual language across the site. I experimented with typography, spacing, and button styles while refining a color palette that balanced clarity with visual interest. Soft neutral backgrounds set a clean foundation, while accent colors were strategically applied to highlight key elements and interactive components, ensuring the design remained cohesive and user-friendly.

Content Inventory

I conducted a content inventory to understand the scope and structure of existing materials and plan how text, images, and multimedia would be organized. This process revealed redundancies and opportunities to clarify each page’s purpose. By prioritizing content placement and establishing a clear hierarchy, I was able to guide the design of responsive layouts that work effectively on both desktop and mobile, ensuring a streamlined and intuitive user experience.
